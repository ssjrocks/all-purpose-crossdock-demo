Message User

Outside Carrier Site Induction

Read and acknowledge these rules before joining the waiting queue.

  1. Remain in the designated waiting area until directed to enter the crossdock.
  2. Wear required PPE, including high-visibility clothing and enclosed safety footwear.
  3. Follow the site speed limit, signs, marked walkways, and staff directions at all times.
  4. Do not enter loading zones or operate loading equipment unless authorised by All Purpose staff.
  5. Use a spotter when directed and take extra care when reversing. Stop immediately if visibility is unclear.
  6. Do not use a handheld phone while driving or moving around operational areas.
  7. Report hazards, damage, spills, injuries, and emergencies immediately to the manager or forklift operator.
